Basic Information

Animal feed is food given to domestic animals in the course of animal husbandry.In agriculture today, the nutritional needs of farm animals are well understood and may be satisfied through natural forage and fodder alone, or augmented by direct supplementation of nutrients in concentrated, controlled form.

The nutritional quality of feed is influenced not only by the nutrient content, but also by many other factors such as feed presentation, hygiene, digestibility, and effect on intestinal health.



There are different types of livestock feeds:

  1. Cattle feeding
  2. Bird food
  3. Cat food
  4. Dog food
  5. Equine nutrition
  6. Pet food
  7. Pig farming
  8. Poultry feed
  9. Sheep husbandry
